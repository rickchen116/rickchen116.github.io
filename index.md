## üìä Features

HydroQC Mini offers various features to assist you in validating your bathymetry data:

- **Quality Reports:** Generates a markdown report containing detailed QA/QC results.
- **JSON Summary:** Provides a summary of the analysis in JSON format for easy integration with other tools.
- **Diagnostic Maps:** Displays maps highlighting depth, missing data, outliers, slope, and roughness.

These features allow you to quickly assess the quality of your data and share your findings with others.
